# 🏠 Home Service Executive Dashboard

### Interactive Business Intelligence Dashboard built with Microsoft Power BI

Transforming Home Service business data into actionable insights through interactive dashboards and executive-level analytics.

---

# 📖 Overview

The **Home Service Executive Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI solution designed for businesses similar to **Urban Company (UrbanClap)**.

The dashboard enables business owners and executives to monitor revenue, customer activity, bookings, service provider performance, and operational KPIs through dynamic visualizations and interactive filters.

It converts raw operational data into meaningful insights that support strategic business decisions.

---

# 🚀 Key Features

### 📊 Executive KPIs

- 💰 Total Revenue
- 👥 Total Customers
- 📅 Total Bookings
- 🧑‍🔧 Total Service Providers
- ⭐ Average Customer Rating
- 💵 Average Order Value

---

### 📈 Analytics

- Monthly Revenue Trend

- Booking Status Distribution

- Service Category
